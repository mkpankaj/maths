\## Mental Maths Topics

- Quiz will cover following topics

  - Addition of up to three numbers (a \<20, b\<20, c\<20)

  - Subtraction (a\<50, b\<50)

  - Multiplication (a\<20, b\<20)

  - Division (a\< 300, b\<20)

  - Divisibility Test for number up to 300

  - Even, Odd, Prime Numbers up to 100

  - Addition and Subtraction of Like Fractions (numerator and denominator \< 20)

  - Comparison of Fractions (numerator and denominator \< 15)

  - Perimeter of Square, Rectangle and Triangle (sides \< 20) 

\## Mental Maths

- Button "Mental Maths" on Home Page on left side of button "My Dashboard" in the Header /Nav bar. 

- It is quiz of 10 questions randomly covering the above topics. Not necessary to cover all the topics. 
- One question at a time on screen with timer.

- Question will stay on screen for 90 sec. After 90 sec, next question will appear.
- if a question is not answered or skipped then mark it "Not Attempted" and give zero marks for that question. 

- Show 04 answers on screen. Only one out of four is correct answer.

- User will select the answer and click submit. Next question will appear

- Application would not show whether answer is correct or wrong after clicking submit.

- At the end of quiz, show the total marks and total time taken for quiz. On the same page show answers and explanations. Also show time taken to solve each questions.
- Stor the quiz details (Date, Time, Marks) for reference later. 
- User can click My Dashboard to see the history of all quizzes under Mental Maths. It will only show Date, Time and Marks. Detailed questions and answers can't be seen from this page.
